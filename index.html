about me..

I’m Nare Epremyan

of Nare Photography. I am a photographer currently in South California. I serve all of Los Angels & surrounding areas.


What I do

capture emotions and tell stories ; hopefully in a meaningful, straight-forward, sometimes subtle or humourous way. I prefer content and moments over gimmicks, fake posing and "creative" compositions : Life is a beautiful journey and work of art, all I do is document it along the way.

My Philosophy


I believe in beautiful. I believe in genuine. I believe in happiness. I believe in light. I believe in creativity. And I believe in love. And that is what I want you to see when you receive your pictures. I want you to see yourself as absolutely stunning, happy, and real.


Let’s create beautiful artwork together...
