Nare Yepremyan

Professional Photographer

Hi there, Im a photographer based on the humongous city of Los Angeles, I capture emotions and tell stories; hopefully in a meaningful, straight-forward, sometimes subtle or humourous way. I prefer content and moments over gimmicks, fake posing and "creative" compositions: Life is a beautiful journey and work of art, all I do is document it along the way.

Services

I work in many events such as: weddings, engages, quincea√±era, birthdays, business meetings, baptism, I also do new born, pregnant, portrait, fashion, animals and family sessions, What makes my job so much fun for me is meeting with different life stories.

My Capturing Philosophy

When it comes to taking an image, I always prefer going out to the nature landscapes, using the best source of light "the nature light", taking an advantage of the nature colors and pattern to add to the spirit of my photos.

My Retouching Philosophy

I do re-touch my photos only my self, I think of them as each single one is a single story, single situation.. some of them will suit with MUCH IS LESS and others will suit LESS IS MUCH.
